Game Mechanics

Basic Controls

Use standard Roblox controls (WASD to move, Space to jump).

Interactable Objects: Hover your mouse over an object and hold the prompted key (0.4s hold). Some objects require touching with your avatar; others work at a short distance.

SoulBind Mechanic

Learnable from Veylen in Chapter 2. Activates during specific boss attacks or chase sequences.

  • Overview: Your body locks, and you control your soul. If your soul takes 3 hits, your vessel shatters (Game Over, full restart).
  • Soul Bar: Shows 3 soul pieces. If hit, a piece is lost, and a timer starts. Avoid hits until the timer runs out to regain a piece (max 3).
  • Soul States:
    • Drift: Normal speed, balanced movement.
    • Echo: Slow soul with afterimages. Attacks start slow, then speed up. Dodge telegraphed slashes.
    • Anchor: Soul locks in place. Use a shield to block attacks every 1â€“2 seconds, or take vessel damage.
  • Note: Without SoulBind, gameplay is harder. Learn it in Chapter 2 for Chapter 3 bosses!
